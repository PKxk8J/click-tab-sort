You can sort tabs from the right click menu on tabs. Sort orders are
  • URL
  • Tile
  • Opened date
  • Accessed date
  • Random
* Opened dates are reset when Firefox starts. * Accessed date orders work with Firefox 56 or later. Pinned tabs are sorted when the right click menu is opened on pinned tabs. Optional notifications show progress. For addons developers: You can use this addon through sendMessage. For details, see README.
